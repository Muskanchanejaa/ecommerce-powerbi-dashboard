# E-Commerce Sales Performance Dashboard

## Problem Statement
An e-commerce retailer operating across 19 Indian states lacked consolidated 
visibility into revenue performance, category profitability, customer payment 
behavior, and regional sales trends.

## Tools Used
Power BI | DAX | Data Modeling | SQL | Excel | CSV

## Dataset
- 1,500 order records across a full fiscal year (2018)
- 336 unique customers across 19 Indian states

## Key Findings
- Total Revenue: ₹4.38L | Total Profit: ₹37K | Profit Margin: 8.4%
- Electronics was the highest revenue category (₹1.66L)
- Printers (₹59.2K) and Sarees (₹59.1K) were top sub-categories by revenue
- Electronic Games was the only loss-making sub-category (₹-644 profit)
- COD was the dominant payment channel at 35.4% of total revenue
- Maharashtra and Madhya Pradesh accounted for the highest order volumes

## Business Impact
- Enabled category-level profitability tracking for inventory decisions
- Payment mode segmentation supported targeted conversion campaigns
- Geo-level drill-down identified regional demand patterns for fulfillment strategy
